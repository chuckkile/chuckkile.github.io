  • History

    Patrick Richardson and Murrayhill Remodeling have been renovating Portland homes since 1997. Patrick has been able to turn one happy client into hundreds of happy clients and has found his passion in building remodels for homeowners throughout the area.

  • Purpose

    Believing that our true purpose on this planet is to realize our potential as humans our approach to each situation is to be all that we can be and do the very best we can. We apply this to remodeling by striving to give the best service and produce the best results surpassing industry standards and raising the bar for remodeling in Portland.

  • Process

    Discover > Design > Plan > Execute. Our process is simple and concise. We help you give us all the information we need and then we take it from there. We handle all phases of construction from design to building. Murrayhill Remodeling is a referral based business so we hope you will be so thrilled with our performance that you'll recommend us to your friends.

  • Network

    Hiring Murrayhill Remodeling gains you access to our expansive network of high-end professionals. We have spent the last 20 years building a team composed of the best tradesman and technicians Portland has to offer. You can rest assured that the folks we bring into your home are trustworthy and masters of their craft.
