  • History

    Murrayhill Remodeling was founded in 1997 by Patrick Richardson. One happy client turned into another and it quickly snowballed to where we are today, helping multiple clients a year turning their house into the home they want. As our company grows so do our capabilities. What started as a one man operation is now a growing team of people bringing top quality remodeling services to the Portland Area.

  • Purpose

    Believing that our true purpose on this planet is to realize our potential as humans our approach to each situation is to be all that we can be. We apply this to remodeling by striving to give the best service and produce the best end results surpassing industry standards and raising the bar for remodeling in Portland.

  • Process

    It all starts with an idea. An idea is turned into a design plan and from there we start planning the execution. A specific schedule is made with a task for each day allowing everyone to know what is happening and when. Once construction is complete we stay in touch to make sure you are completely satisfied. Any issues are handled by us immediately. In the future we hope you are pleased with the experience and will recomend us to your friends.

  • Method

    We prefer to work with interior designers and architects when projects meet a certain complexity threshold. This allows our customers to work with professionals whose job it is to bring designs to life on a screen. After design preferences are chosen and a plan is made, we bring those designs to life.
